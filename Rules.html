Rules

  • ⚑
    The use of x-ray or x-ray texture packs is strictly forbidden.
  • ⚑
    You may swear as long as its not directed at other players.
  • ⚑
    Use of ANY offensive slur is not allowed.
  • ⚑
    Topics such as; suicide, real life politics/religion are not allowed.
  • ⚑
    Adult chat topics are only allowed during a grace period from 12am to 6am EST.
  • ⚑
    Intentionally lagging down the server by any means is forbidden.
  • ⚑
    If you are found with a laggy farm you will be asked to tear it down.
  • ⚑
    While rail, tnt and carpet duping is allowed all other forms of duping is not allowed.
  • ⚑
    Please use common sense.
  • ⚑
    Treat what staff say as a rule.

Allowed Mods (generalized)

  1. Performance enhancing mods such as sodium or optifine.
  2. Brightness boosting mods such as full bright.
  3. Schematic mods such as schematica/litematica (printer mode is not allowed).
  4. Mini-maps/world maps (cave maps and entity radar is allowed).
  5. Plasmo Voice mod (required to use proximity chat).
  6. Better HUD mods.

(All other mods must be approved by staff)
(If you are unsure about a specific mod contact any staff mod+)
